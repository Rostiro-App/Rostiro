You are helping me build the external social media and launch marketing system for Rostiro, my fantasy sports operating system.

Important context:

Rostiro is not ready for heavy conversion campaigns yet. The NFL season is about 1.5 months away, so the current goal is:

1. Grow awareness
2. Build trust
3. Attract early signups and beta users
4. Start making the product feel real in public
5. Create repeatable content systems for launch season
6. Establish Rostiro as a serious fantasy football product before the season starts

Do not over-optimize for paid conversions yet. We are building the audience, waitlist, early user base, and credibility.

Your job:

Audit the current product, PRD, codebase, app screens, feature set, and brand kit exactly as they currently exist. Do not make assumptions that conflict with the PRD or product. Use what already exists.

The strategy I want you to follow:

X will probably become one of the most important platforms. Use it for build updates, screenshots, GIFs, feature reveals, NFL news reactions, Product Hunt launch content, founder thoughts, and daily posts.

TikTok is likely the biggest growth opportunity. This is not about dancing or trends. Focus on screen recordings, fantasy tips, feature reveals, live Sunday clips, product demos, and "manage all your teams in one place" pain points.

Instagram should focus on Reels, reused TikToks, Sunday Stories, carousel posts, and fantasy-football-specific product content.

YouTube should focus on Shorts first. Every TikTok should also become a Short. Eventually feature walkthroughs, launch videos, and roadmap videos.

Reddit should not be spammed. Do not create a subreddit yet. The goal is to become known and helpful in communities like r/fantasyfootball, r/DynastyFF, r/FFCommish, and r/SleeperApp. Create value-first post ideas and comment strategies that do not sound promotional.

Discord should be created even if it only has a few users. It should become the place for bug reports, feature requests, announcements, beta testing, and early-user feedback.

Product Hunt matters for credibility, not because we expect massive user volume. Build a launch plan and asset checklist.

LinkedIn should be used for the founder story, investor credibility, engineers, potential hires, recruiters, and proof that this is a serious product.

Bluesky and Threads can mostly be cross-post channels.

Newsletter should eventually be important. Prefer Beehiiv, not Mailchimp. Content should include waivers, injuries, product updates, weekly fantasy pain points, and feature announcements.

Core product positioning to emphasize:

Rostiro helps fantasy players manage all of their fantasy teams in one place.

Pain points to focus on:

No more switching between apps on Sundays.
All of your fantasy teams, managed in one place.
All of your leagues, all of your players, all together.
Sunday should not feel like opening ten tabs just to know who is playing.
One clean place for every roster, matchup, injury, score, and update.
Fantasy managers need a command center, not another app to check.

Avoid generic copy like:
"Win your league"
"Dominate fantasy football"
"Next-gen fantasy platform"
"Revolutionary fantasy experience"

Make the copy more specific, user-pain-driven, and Sunday-focused.
